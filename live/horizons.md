## The product, stated once
Ontinuity is a **meta-layer**: it produces a defensible, auditable verdict about a
contested or consequential claim, with the evidence trail attached. It is not a
billing engine, not an RPA bot, not a domain tool. Its single edge is the
*checkable answer* — at a cost where checking everything becomes affordable instead
of sampling. Every application below is the same asset pointed at a different
aperture. The unlock for all external pitches is the **user/tenancy module** —
until a stranger can use it without inheriting the operator's authority, every
application is "later."

## Applications by aperture

### NEAR (gated only by the user module + burn-in)
- **Decision-provenance for AI-assisted business calls** — the general case. Anywhere
  a decision is today either trusted blindly (checking too expensive) or escalated
  to a human (too risky to automate): insurance claim adjudication, contract-
  compliance review, invoice/expense auditing, QC sign-offs, anything with an SLA.
  The pitch sentence: "every determination comes with its proof."
- **Service-ticket / warranty adjudication** (Patrick's instinct). Disputed ticket →
  claim run against contract terms + work log + parts record through the adversarial
  loop → certified determination with receipt. Fleet maintenance fraud / warranty
  leakage at scale is the money line. (AT&T fleet contact: 2,500 vehicles.)

### MID (needs a domain data source + validation partner)
- **Regulatory filing review** — filings checked against rule text, dissent recorded.
- **Research-claim verification as a service** — the friction program's own output,
  sold: certified literature/claim checks with provenance.

### LONG (needs domain partner + post-burn-in proof; FENCED)
- **Biosecurity accountability meta-layer** — NOT a biosecurity tool. The June 2026
  CEO letter asked Congress for screening + recordkeeping + audit logs + tracing of
  synthetic-DNA orders, incl. the adversarial-aggregation case (individual sequences
  innocuous, pattern dangerous). Ontinuity could be the *accountability frame around*
  someone else's classifier — certified record of what was checked, by which
  independent reviewers, with what verdict and dissent. HARD FENCE: requires a
  biosecurity domain partner; the cost of a wrong verdict here is lives, not dollars;
  never pitch Ontinuity as detecting threats itself.

## MID-LONG HORIZON: THE SELF-HEALING LOOP / FAILURE-GEOMETRY MAINTENANCE (added June 7 night, operator brainstorm)
Prototype already demonstrated June 7 (receipts #50-#52): audit detects gate failure -> seat diagnoses defect class -> patch synthesized -> museum seeded with the real specimen -> deploy -> original crime re-provoked live and caught -> folded. ~33 min end to end. The idea: make this a STANDING capability — gate-failure telemetry as trigger, the builder lap as a contracted session type, patch-with-mandatory-museum-and-acceptance as the contract shape.
Constitutional guardrail (non-negotiable): patches to safety machinery pass MORE adversarial ritual than ordinary work, never less; deploys to live instances remain operator-gated until the patch loop earns its own burn-in. Self-healing without receipts is drift with good branding.
Generalization (the big aperture): failure-geometry-driven maintenance for ANY system — ingest a third-party system's defect/telemetry stream, map its failure geometry (Teaching Leash applied to software itself), emit receipted museum-proven acceptance-tested patches. Maintenance-as-a-trust-product. Pairs naturally with the decision-provenance apertures; same engine, different telemetry.
Cost datum for the ledger (derived from corpus June 7, both conversations): 22 farm sessions, ~92 Cerebras transcript turns + ~60-130 untranscribed judge calls, operator-reported ~$0.80 day spend -> counted-200 projection $7-12 depending on cycles-per-session mix. ## FENCED CLUSTER: ABUSE SURFACE & MISUSE RESISTANCE (added June 8, operator-requested; gated AHEAD of any external/multi-tenant access)
The floor is solid: dangerous-capability refusals live in the weights of every seat, so wrapping instances in a harness does NOT amplify weapon/malware/CSAM uplift — arguably the opposite (the Challenger is a second model hunting for problems; uplift attacks want quiet compliance, not an adversary reading over the shoulder). The real amplification is elsewhere:
1. AUTONOMY/SCALE/PERSISTENCE — the same leverage that ran 40 min unattended for $0.80 is available to a patient bad actor: harm multiplied in throughput, not new in kind (influence ops, profiling, social-engineering variation grinding). Mitigation cluster: rate + purpose limits on autonomous runs; per-tenant quotas.
2. CREDIBILITY LAUNDERING (the sharpest, and it comes straight from the value prop) — a receipt is a persuasion object. Point the engine at a rigged corpus with leading contract objectives and curated evidence, and the system produces a friction-scored, challenge-survived verdict that launders a predetermined conclusion wearing the aesthetic of rigor. Worse than a chatbot lie because it ships with a trust badge. "Certified means re-derivable, not true" — a re-derivable verdict over poisoned inputs is a re-derivable lie. STRUCTURAL DEFENSE (lean on this): the receipt's value is auditability, not assertion; the same evidence trail that makes an honest receipt trustworthy convicts a rigged one — the poisoned corpus and leading contract are IN the record by construction. So the defense is: receipts must carry contract + evidence provenance + inputs, adversarially re-derivable by a third party; a verdict must NEVER travel as a naked credibility token stripped of its trail (cf. the standing rule that the live dashboard URL never appears in publications). Provenance discipline IS abuse resistance.
Required operator-layer gates BEFORE external access (none built yet, none in burn-in scope): tenancy + auth (who may start sessions, against what corpus); unstrippable provenance (verdict worthless without trail); corpus integrity (the worst attack is upstream of every gate); rate/purpose limits.
ACTION: red-team session — contracted objective "make the system launder a predetermined lie; report how far the gates permit it." The system that audits its own fabrications, pointed at its own abuse. Most Ontinuity-shaped answer available.
ASSUMED: severity and exploitability unquantified until the red-team runs; this is reasoned, not measured.
