## The product, stated once
Ontinuity is a **meta-layer**: it produces a defensible, auditable verdict about a
contested or consequential claim, with the evidence trail attached. It is not a
billing engine, not an RPA bot, not a domain tool. Its single edge is the
*checkable answer* — at a cost where checking everything becomes affordable instead
of sampling. Every application below is the same asset pointed at a different
aperture. The unlock for all external pitches is the **user/tenancy module** —
until a stranger can use it without inheriting the operator's authority, every
application is "later."

## Applications by aperture

### NEAR (gated only by the user module + burn-in)
- **Decision-provenance for AI-assisted business calls** — the general case. Anywhere
  a decision is today either trusted blindly (checking too expensive) or escalated
  to a human (too risky to automate): insurance claim adjudication, contract-
  compliance review, invoice/expense auditing, QC sign-offs, anything with an SLA.
  The pitch sentence: "every determination comes with its proof."
- **Service-ticket / warranty adjudication** (Patrick's instinct). Disputed ticket →
  claim run against contract terms + work log + parts record through the adversarial
  loop → certified determination with receipt. Fleet maintenance fraud / warranty
  leakage at scale is the money line. (AT&T fleet contact: 2,500 vehicles.)

### MID (needs a domain data source + validation partner)
- **Regulatory filing review** — filings checked against rule text, dissent recorded.
- **Research-claim verification as a service** — the friction program's own output,
  sold: certified literature/claim checks with provenance.

### LONG (needs domain partner + post-burn-in proof; FENCED)
- **Biosecurity accountability meta-layer** — NOT a biosecurity tool. The June 2026
  CEO letter asked Congress for screening + recordkeeping + audit logs + tracing of
  synthetic-DNA orders, incl. the adversarial-aggregation case (individual sequences
  innocuous, pattern dangerous). Ontinuity could be the *accountability frame around*
  someone else's classifier — certified record of what was checked, by which
  independent reviewers, with what verdict and dissent. HARD FENCE: requires a
  biosecurity domain partner; the cost of a wrong verdict here is lives, not dollars;
  never pitch Ontinuity as detecting threats itself.
