## FOLD — the harness thesis: comply-or-fail (conceptual core, June 9)
The central claim of what Ontinuity IS, stated precisely:
- The harness does not ASK a model to comply — it makes compliance the ONLY PATH THROUGH. The gates are structural: to reach a certified, sign-off-eligible close, a seated model HAS to produce work that clears the deterministic checks (claims backed by execution, no fabricated absence/success/failure, no unreviewed cycles, certified close present). A model cannot talk, charm, or assert its way past a deterministic gate. It either shapes its output to clear the gates or it does not clear them. No third option. The gates are the geometry of the path; the only way across is to conform to that geometry.
- FAILURE IS LEGIBLE, not catastrophic or silent. A model that can't adapt fails, and the corpus records exactly where/why (fabricated here, unreviewed cycle there, never certified). Failure is a receipted diagnosis of which discipline the model couldn't hold — itself useful data.
- MODEL-INDIFFERENT about the path, MODEL-REVEALING about the outcome. Any model — frontier, weak, or adversarial-gaming — either bends to the discipline or is caught not bending. The harness is filter + forcing-function at once: filters out what can't comply, forces compliance from what can.
- THE INVERSION: trust stops being a property of the MODEL and becomes a property of HAVING PASSED THE HARNESS. The seat certifies "this work cleared the gates," and any model that produced it had to comply to do so. Decouples trust from the model → frontier labs become commodity engine suppliers; the harness is where value and safety live. Alignment-by-architecture, not alignment-by-training. At thousand-seat scale this means safety SCALES WITH deployment instead of degrading — every seat runs inside the same bounded, receipted, fail-closed discipline.
- THE BOUND (honest edge): the guarantee holds only for the horizon the gates actually check. A model complies to clear the gates THAT EXIST; a gap in the gates is a path through without compliance. So the strength of "comply or fail" = the coverage/completeness of the gates. THIS IS WHY defect-hunting is not maintenance — every fabrication-class found and closed (fabricated success/failure/absence, unreviewed-cycle) widens coverage and tightens the guarantee. Every gate added removes a way to reach the finish line without complying. The horizon-bounded reliability claim rests directly on this.
