## 1. The living mark (design, locked over many screenshot iterations)
Operator drove a frame-by-frame visual design of the Ontinuity mark: concentric hex-PAIRS
(gold stroke only, never filled, never octagonal — corpus rule), 3D axis-spin (outer 46s,
inner counter 17s), directional shine on the rotating lines, an intentional coherence/
decoherence cycle the operator chose to KEEP ("resembles life at its truest"), self-feeding
feeder particles, a pulsing core, and a gravitational-lens background grid.
Key rulings (verbatim intent): glow "feels unnatural... a single color creating a haze without
depth" → rebuilt as a layered warm radial that shifts hue on falloff. Then "the shine is too
intense... center of the glow is too far to the right... center it on the logo... more subtle
and not rhyme with the logo color" → glow recentred 53%, dimmed, hue offset to bronze so the
gold lines stay distinct; shine on the rotating lines kept. Grid: "the grid lines behind the
logo do not line up with the grid lines on the site background... the lensing needs to be more
prominent... the grid needs to sink in behind the logo seamlessly... if it pulsed in and out
slightly, that would give it more realism" → replaced the separate SVG grid with ONE canvas
grid phase-aligned to the page's 48px grid, gravitationally warped toward the mark, sinking
behind it, pulsing the lens mass ±18%. Operator verdict: "The logo is mesmerizing!"
